My Service

I provide comprehensive IT services specializing in web and mobile app, UX/UI design, and database management. My expertise covers both front-end and back-end development.

Website Developer

A Website Developer builds and maintains websites, ensuring they are visually appealing, functional, and optimized for performance. They work on both the front-end (user interface) and back-end (server-side logic) using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and databases. Their focus includes responsiveness, SEO, and security to deliver smooth, reliable user experiences across different devices.

Mobile Developer

A Mobile Developer creates apps for mobile devices, focusing on either native (iOS, Android) or cross-platform development (React Native, Flutter). They ensure that apps are optimized for different screen sizes, network conditions, and battery life. Mobile developers also focus on performance, app security, and delivering regular updates to keep apps running smoothly on mobile platforms.

UX/UI Designer

A UX/UI Designer ensures digital products are both functional and visually engaging. UI designers focus on the aesthetics—layout, colors, and typography—while UX designers improve usability through research, wireframing, and prototyping. They work to create user-friendly, intuitive products that provide a seamless experience and align with brand goals.
